Amanda Boles

Many Practices struggle with systems, protocols, and accountability.

With a career in dentistry that spans over 30 years and having worked several positions in the practice, Amanda Boles understands the obstacles that dental professionals face daily. She believes it is important for dental professionals to know that they are not alone and that there are proven systems and resources that can help them reach their goals!

Amanda has worn many hats including those of RDA/EFDA, treatment coordinator, financial coordinator, biller, and office manager. Drawing from her vast experience, Amanda's programs provide a custom roadmap and have been implemented in practices across the country with strong results: doubling production, improving collections, organizing workflow to maximize productivity, building positive and valued teams, and increasing new patients all without outside marketing.

Amand is passionate about dentistry and loves helping dental professionals achieve their full potential. As a practice management coach and certified Dentrix trainer, Amanda relates well with all members of the team and she brings the knowledge, experience, and skills to help them achieve practice goals.
